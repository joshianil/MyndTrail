Good Parenting using Theatre

Parenting is not just about giving instructions — it's about understanding, connecting, and communicating with your child. Theatre-based parenting workshops offer a creative, engaging way for parents to explore real-life situations and emotions in a safe, guided space.

How It Helps: Through role-play, storytelling, and expressive techniques, parents gain deeper empathy, learn active listening, and discover practical ways to handle challenges at home. It encourages self-reflection and improves parent-child bonding.

Why You Should Attend: This session is designed to help you build stronger, more respectful relationships with your children. Whether you're a new parent or have years of experience, this interactive event will leave you with fresh perspectives and tools to support your parenting journey.
